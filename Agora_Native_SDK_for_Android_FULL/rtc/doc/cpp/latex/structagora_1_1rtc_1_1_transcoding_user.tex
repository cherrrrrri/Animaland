\item 0\+: (default) Supports dual channels at most, depending on the upstream of the broadcaster.

\item 3\+: The audio stream of the broadcaster is in the FR audio channel. If the upstream of the broadcaster uses dual sound channel, only the left sound channel is used for streaming.
\item 4\+: The audio stream of the broadcaster is in the BL audio channel. If the upstream of the broadcaster uses dual sound channel, only the left sound channel is used for streaming.
