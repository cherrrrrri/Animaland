Reports the face detection result of the local user. Applies to Android and i\+OS only.

Once you enable face detection by calling set\+Camera\+Face\+Detection (true), you can get the following information on the local user in real-\/time\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item The width and height of the local video.
\item The position of the human face in the local video.
\item The distance between the human face and the device screen. This value is based on the fitting calculation of the local video size and the position of the human face.
\end{DoxyItemize}

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item If the S\+DK does not detect a face, it reduces the frequency of this callback to reduce power consumption on the local device.
\item The S\+DK stops triggering this callback when a human face is in close proximity to the screen.
\item On Android, the distance value reported in this callback may be slightly different from the actual distance. Therefore, Agora does not recommend using it for accurate calculation.
\end{DoxyItemize}
\end{DoxyNote}
